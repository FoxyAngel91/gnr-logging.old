What We do

With a dozen employees operating over 30 pieces of equipment, our main focus is gravel hauling. Presently there are nine gravel trucks and two loaders operating in Garvin Pits, located north of the city of Winnipeg. Ours other equipment includes a dozer, hoe, buncher, slasher, skidders, a variety of trailers including a flat bed and log trailer as well as 2 picker trucks and 2 semis. Owner and Operator Gilles Nault runs the business from his home in Richer, Manitoba.

How It Started...

When the company first started it was called G&R Skidding. We had only two employees, Gilles and Raymond, and we did all of the work with only a chain-saw and a skidder. In 1997 we expanded and bought Chevrefil Forest Products, a contracting company based out of Pine Falls. Along with more equipment, the acquisition also included a contract with the Abitibi Consolidated Pulp & Paper Mill. Soon after we also began supplying the Kenora Weyerhaeuser mill (now known as the Trus Joist TimberStrand Plant). Later we incorporated and became G&R Logging.
